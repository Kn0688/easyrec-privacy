Privacy Policy

EasyRec · On-Device Speech-to-Text

Last updated: July 20, 2026

EasyRec (“the App”) is developed by Guodong Fei. We take your privacy seriously. In one sentence: the App runs entirely on your device — it does not connect to the internet, upload anything, or collect any of your data.

1. We Collect No Data

The App contains no networking code. It never sends your recordings, transcribed text, or any personal information to us or any third party. You can verify this yourself in iOS under Settings → Privacy & Security → App Privacy Report: the App shows no network activity.

2. Where Your Data Lives

  • Recordings and transcripts are stored only in your device’s local storage, fully under your control.
  • Speech recognition runs entirely via on-device offline AI models; audio never leaves your device.
  • We cannot and will not access any of your content.

3. Microphone Access

The App requests microphone access to record audio. Recorded audio is used solely for on-device transcription and is never uploaded. You may revoke this permission anytime in system settings.

4. Crash Logs (Optional, Shared Only by You)

If the App crashes, a log is stored only on your device to help diagnose the issue. These logs are never sent automatically. They leave your device only if you choose to share them from the “About” screen, via a method you pick (e.g. email) — whether and to whom to send is entirely your decision. You can also clear these logs anytime within the App.

5. Third Parties

The App integrates no third-party advertising, analytics, or tracking SDKs. It uses several open-source speech-recognition models and libraries, all of which run locally on your device with no data transmission. Full attribution and license texts are available on the Open-Source Licenses page.

6. Children’s Privacy

The App is not directed at children under 13 and does not knowingly collect personal information from children (in fact, it collects no information from anyone).

7. Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes, we will revise this page and update the “Last updated” date above.

8. Contact Us

For any questions about this Privacy Policy, contact: 331665286@qq.com

隐私政策

易识 EasyRec · 离线语音转文字

最后更新：2026 年 7 月 20 日

易识（EasyRec，下称“本应用”）由 Guodong Fei 开发。我们高度重视你的隐私。一句话概括：本应用完全在你的设备上运行，不联网、不上传、不收集你的任何数据。

1. 我们不收集任何数据

本应用不含任何网络代码，不会将你的录音、转写文字、或任何个人信息发送到我们或任何第三方的服务器。你可以在 iOS 系统的“设置 → 隐私与安全性 → App 隐私报告”中自行核实：本应用没有任何网络活动。

2. 你的数据存在哪里

  • 录音与转写文字：仅保存在你的设备本地存储中，由你完全掌控。
  • 语音识别：全部由设备本地的离线 AI 模型完成，音频不会离开你的设备。
  • 我们无法访问、也不会访问你的任何内容。

3. 麦克风权限

本应用需要麦克风权限以进行录音。录制的音频仅用于在设备本地转写为文字，绝不上传。你可随时在系统设置中撤销该权限。

4. 崩溃日志（可选、由你主动分享）

若应用发生崩溃，日志会仅保存在你的设备本地，用于帮助排查问题。这些日志不会自动发送。仅当你主动在“关于”页选择分享时，才会通过你选择的方式（如邮件）发出——是否发送、发给谁，完全由你决定。你也可随时在应用内清除这些日志。

5. 第三方

本应用不集成任何第三方广告、分析或追踪 SDK。应用使用了若干开源语音识别模型与库，它们均在你的设备本地运行，不涉及任何数据传输。完整署名与许可证全文见开源许可页面。

6. 儿童隐私

本应用不面向 13 岁以下儿童，也不会有意收集儿童的个人信息（事实上不收集任何人的信息）。

7. 政策变更

若本政策有更新，我们会在此页面修订并更新顶部的“最后更新”日期。

8. 联系我们

如对本隐私政策有任何疑问，请联系：331665286@qq.com
